Sava Residence is a modern residential and commercial complex located at Ustanička Street №. 12, ready for occupancy in September 2025. The building features a structure of 4G+P+7+Ps and consists of two sections with a total of 208 residential units, carefully designed to achieve optimal functionality, brightness, and luxury-class technical equipment.

Sava Residence is your home

The complex brings a revolution in construction, starting with a realistic assessment of your living needs.

The apartments, with picturesque views reaching all corners of the world, serve as a gateway between Belgrade's cherished districts, Vracar and Vozdovac, highlighting the complex's strategic location.

The open-concept living and dining area fosters warmth and connection, complemented by Lux category interiors featuring premium materials, exquisite details, and impeccable taste.

All apartments have spacious terraces and high windows designed to maximize use of light and an unobstructed view. With special attention, we have provided you with a spacious environment that will provide you with a pleasant atmosphere in the space that will represent your new home.

A spacious and elegant lobby welcomes guests and residents, creating a cozy and stylish atmosphere from the very first step.

Business apartments are located in Lamela B, there are 19 business apartm ents on the first floor.
We have provided different stru c tu re s of business apartments, with one, two or three bedrooms, which can be the perfect choice for your business activity that can be carried out in your home.
Business apartm ents provide the perfect possibility of living and doing business in one place. Take the opportunity to secure this type of property.

On the ground floor of the building there are 6 shops of different square meters, which in total occupy an area of 1.946m2. Lamellas a belong to 3 shops that make up a total area of 746m2, while lamellas B consists of a larger area intended for shops, 1200m2 distributed on 3 large locations.


They are oriented northeast, in the direction of Ustanička Street. They are suitable for various types of Service and commercial activities, which will make life for future tenants of the complex even more comfortable.


The premises are supplied to future owners with shell and core, with no finishes on the walls and floors.

On the basement floor and three underground levels there are a total of 261 parking spaces. The largest number of parking spaces has an area of 12.5 m², while for people with disabilities are 18.5 m².

Fire Protection is provided through internal and external hydrants and sprinkler systems. Ventilation and dehumidification of garages is performed with the help of jet-fan fans and axial roof fans.

Entrance to the garage is provided by Access Control and segmental Rollo doors and ramps. LED lights with motion sensors and panic lamps are used for lighting. Communication from each level takes place by stairs and elevators.

All these parameters provide your vehicle with protection and safety, and you with a peaceful sleep.

Materials

FACADE

The part that is designed as a ventilated facade consists of thermal insulation and composite panels on the base. The part that is designed as a contact facade consists of thermal insulation and finishing

CONSTRUCTIVE SYSTEM

The structural system is reinforced concrete

FACADE LOCKSMITH

Facade hardware is made of aluminum profiles with thermal break, three-layer glass package, German fittings, aluminum blinds with electric motor

FENCES TERRACE

Terrace fences are made of tempered glass

ELECTRIC LIFT

Electronic lift maker KONE MonoSpace, lift speed 1.75m/s

ENTRANCE DOOR

The entrance door is of metal construction, the wing finish is mediapane painted with polyurethane paint. Equipped with a safety lock

ROOM DOOR

The wing has a total thickness of 42mm coated with mediapane painted polyurethane paint

Heating

Central with a connection to the city heating plant, with calorimeters for each residential unit. The radiators are aluminium bimetallic. In the bathrooms are provided towel dryers (tube radiators)

Air conditioning

split system with one interior unit per apartment. Masks are provided for the outdoor unit; on the retractable floor a multi split system is provided with an indoor unit in each room masks are provided for the outdoor unit

Parquet

On the floors in the rooms is parquet 14 mm thick

CERAMIC TILES

On the floors and walls in the bathrooms and kitchen are provided high-quality Italian ceramic tiles made by Marazzi

SANITARY EQUIPMENT

toilet cups and sinks are VILLEROY& BOCH, bath KOLPA sleep, water heater ARISTON, shower cabins are brand HUPPE, batteries are HANSGROHE
